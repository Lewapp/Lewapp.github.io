Abyssal Dread

About

​Abyssal Dread began as a third-year university project and was submitted for the Games Academy Expo 2023. After the initial submission, we continued to enhance the game by incorporating additional features and refining gameplay elements. This dedication led to the game receiving the People's Choice Award and the Most Cohesive Art Style Award at the expo.

Project Info

Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Team Size: 7
Role: Gameplay Programmer
Development Time: 8 Months
Project Type: Year 3 University

Test Room

This room was built to help the team develop, test and bug fix mechanics. In this room you could become invincible, teleport, spawn and clear any enemy type. There was also the ability to fly around.

I developed three distinct enemy behaviour types: close-range attackers (Thralls and Tanks), ranged evaders (Yeeters), and aggressive chargers/jumpers (Skullcrabs and Dogs).

Enemy Dismemberment System

I implemented a straightforward dismemberment system that assigned individual health values to each enemy limb. When an enemy was hit, damage was applied to a random limb, and if its health reached zero, the limb would be removed and replaced with a corresponding dismembered version at the same location.
